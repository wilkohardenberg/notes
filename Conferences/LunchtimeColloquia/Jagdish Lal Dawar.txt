Conserving Natural Resources: indigenous knowledge...

Institution of Patang: cooperative system of reciprocal generosity. Each young apatani becomes member of a patang, and will help her patang friends in case they need help for any agricultural (or other) work. Also general social activities are performed jointly.

Cooperative spirit maintained through social and religious institutions. Unquestioning following of certain forms of social conduct + 2 religious festivals
